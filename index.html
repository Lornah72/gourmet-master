About Gourmet Master

At Gourmet Master, we specialize in curating and delivering authentic Italian food experiences to Kenyan businesses. From imported cheeses and pastas to consultation services for restaurants, hotels, and retailers, we make Italian excellence accessible and profitable.

With branches in Nairobi and Mombasa, we work hand-in-hand with our clients to source the finest products, train kitchen staff, and even help you launch your own Italian-themed business.
