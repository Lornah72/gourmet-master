About Gourmet Master

Gourmet Master Ltd is a premium distributor of fine food and beverages in Kenya. With roots tracing back to Italian heritage and a passion for culinary excellence, we proudly serve homes, restaurants, and hospitality providers across the country.

Our Story

Founded by Roberto and Renata, Gourmet Master was born out of a desire to bring authentic gourmet products to Kenyan households and the hospitality industry. From humble beginnings in Mombasa, we've grown to become a trusted name, known for quality, trust, and dedication.

Our commitment to excellence and deep relationships with suppliers have enabled us to source and deliver premium goods with consistency and care. Whether it's cheese, wine, pasta, or cold cuts – we bring the taste of tradition and innovation to your table.
