Our Story

Over 30 years ago, Roberto Miano arrived in Kenya and fell in love with its vibrant culture and people. Inspired by his passion for authentic Italian cuisine, he brought the essence of Italy to Kenya, from fine cheeses and wines to truffles and espresso. Together with Renata, their shared vision led to the birth of Gourmet Master Ltd, now a leading name in Kenya for premium Italian products and B2B consultation.

Today, Gourmet Master is more than a distributor, we are culinary partners to hotels, restaurants, and retail shops across Kenya. Whether you're starting an Italian restaurant or sourcing hard-to-find delicacies, we offer tailored support, product expertise, and a taste of Italy, right here in Kenya.

We don’t just supply , we guide, train, and inspire.
